Our History

Lochquarry Outdoor Centre has been providing unforgettable outdoor experiences for children and young people for over [X] years. Located in the heart of nature, we offer a wide range of activities designed to inspire, challenge, and build confidence in kids aged 6 to 18. From hill walking and archery to bushcraft and team-building exercises, our activities are tailored to develop key life skills while having fun.

In addition to our programs for children and youth, we also arrange activities for parents, guardians, or carers who are looking for engaging and educational experiences for their children. These activities can be personalized to suit the needs of any group, and we also cater to groups of individuals over 18, providing team-building and leadership activities for adult groups as well.

We work closely with schools, charities, and organizations dedicated to supporting youth development. Through our collaborative efforts, we offer group activities that foster teamwork, resilience, and personal growth, ensuring every participant—regardless of age—leaves with valuable skills and memories that last a lifetime.

At Lochquarry, we believe in the power of the outdoors to shape and inspire minds of all ages. Our team of experienced instructors is committed to creating a safe, inclusive, and enriching environment where people of all ages can explore, learn, and thrive.
